So… what is a computer?

A computer is a machine that takes in information, follows instructions, and then shows a result.

It can do this really fast. Like… faster-than-you-can-blink fast.

A quick story

Imagine you press a button that says “Play Music”.

The computer:

  • Notices the button was pressed
  • Follows the “play music” instructions
  • Plays the music

The computer didn’t decide to play music. It just followed the rules it was given.

What can computers do?

Computers can do loads of things, like:

  • Play games
  • Show videos
  • Help with homework
  • Draw pictures
  • Remember lots of things
  • Count super fast

But here’s the big secret: a computer only does these things because people wrote instructions for it.

Try this: “Be the computer”

Grab a grown-up or a friend.

Rules: The “computer” must do exactly what it’s told. No guessing!

Try instructions like:

  • Clap twice
  • Turn around
  • Take three steps forward
  • Draw a circle

You’ll notice computers are powerful… but only because people tell them what to do.

The big idea

A computer is not magic.

It’s a machine that follows instructions to help people do things.
