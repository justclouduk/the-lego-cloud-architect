Who Built This Place?

Hi! I’m the LEGO Cloud Architect. I’ve been working with computers for over 25 years — which in tech years means I am approximately 147 years old.

I started back when computers were slower, heavier, louder, and much less cool than they are today. For the last 15 years I’ve been working in “the cloud” — which is just a fancy way of saying “other people’s really big computers.”

I live with my amazing wife and my two brilliant boys. They ask me questions like: “How does the internet work?” “Where do videos live?” “Can a computer think?”

This website exists because of them. They are my testers, my critics, my idea machines, and sometimes my tech support when I forget what button I just clicked.

My goal is simple: help kids understand technology without big scary words — and maybe inspire some future builders, inventors, and cloud architects along the way.
